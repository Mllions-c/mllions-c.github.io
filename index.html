Probing-Guided Adversarial Suffix Attacks for Evaluating LLM Robustness

PROBIX is an adversarial suffix attack framework guided by internal model representations. It designs structured suffixes (composed of connector phrases and adversarial sequences) and optimizes them through a composite objective that jointly considers attack effectiveness, semantic similarity, and linguistic fluency (measured by perplexity). By incorporating task-specific linear probing and a POS-aware diversification strategy, PROBIX significantly enhances the stealthiness and efficacy of adversarial attacks, outperforming state-of-the-art methods across multiple benchmark datasets.
